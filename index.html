About Us

Coffeemania is an independent and locally owned coffee shop that opened in 2016.

We pride ourselves on bringing the best coffee to the heart of Baku City. We also offer a daily assortment of fresh baked goods and our Award Winning Chocolate Chip Cookies.

Coffeemania is a dedicated multi-roaster, supplying a rotating menu of coffees from some of the best roasters all across the country. In addition to brewed coffee and espresso drinks, we offer a selection of teas and baked goods.

We love coffee and we want to share our passion with you. Come in and try your favorite coffee drinks or give our seasonal specials a try. Make sure to follow us on Instagram to keep up to date with our newest offerings.

We are open daily 7am-5pm.

We are located at 46e Ashig Molla Juma, Baku.
